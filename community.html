Environment

Fortem lives with a tribe deep in a jungle. There are several tribes is this jungle. Most of the most part, these tribes at peace with each other. Weather is sunny and warm during the day, but night is ice cold. For the most part the jungle is quiet. You can hear birds chirping and flying around. but from time to time you hear drones flying around. You can also hear booms when members of the tribes engage with these drones.
